⚠ Single Point of Failure — The Risk That Grows With Every New Project

Every system above runs through one person. Based on the hours entered, an unplanned 5-day absence leaves — hours of unprocessed operational data. Returns go unlogged. Requests pile up. The weekly report doesn't get built. As Impact takes on more projects, this risk doesn't stay the same — it compounds.
