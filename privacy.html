Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

What we collect

When you submit a form on this site — the contact form, the ROI audit, or the profit assessment — we collect the information you enter: your name, email address, practice type, and the description of your process. We also collect the inputs you provide to the ROI calculator if you choose to submit them.

How we use it

Solely to respond to your inquiry. We use your information to follow up on the specific question or request you submitted. We do not add you to a mailing list, send marketing emails, or contact you about anything other than what you reached out about.

Who we share it with

No one. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their use. Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a secure form backend, for delivery to our inbox. Formspree's privacy policy governs their handling of that transmission.

Your client data

If we engage in a project together, any client data involved in the systems we build stays in your environment — on infrastructure you control. We do not store, copy, or retain your clients' information on Valore-owned servers.

Questions

If you have questions about this policy or want to request deletion of your information, use the contact form on the main site.
