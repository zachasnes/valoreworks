Questions

What people usually ask first.

A few things worth knowing before you fill out the form.

Do I have to get on a call to get the assessment?

No. That's the point. You fill out the form, we do the work, and we send you a written assessment within 48 hours. If the numbers look interesting to you, we can book a call — but only if you want to. You'll get the full assessment either way.

What happens after I receive the assessment?

Nothing, unless you want something to happen. We'll include a link to book a call if you want to go deeper, but there's no automatic follow-up sequence or sales cadence. If you're interested, you reach out. If you're not, you still keep the assessment.

How accurate is the ROI estimate without a full audit?

Directionally accurate. The form gives us enough to produce a grounded estimate — industry, team size, process type, and time spent are the main variables. The number will be conservative. Once we talk and get into the specifics of your workflow, it usually goes up, not down.

What if my situation doesn't fit any of the options in the form?

Pick the closest option and note it when you enter your name — or just reach out directly through the contact form on the main site. We work across a range of industries and processes. If we've seen something similar, we'll say so in the assessment.

Is this really free?

Yes. No credit card, no trial period, no gotcha. The assessment is genuinely useful to you whether or not you ever hire us — and the best way to earn your trust is to give you something worth having before we ask for anything.
